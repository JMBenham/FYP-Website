This website is designed to allow teachers to understand the different types of devices that are available. The rating system is out of 5 stars. They are averaged from the responses given by surveys returned by teachers. Only answers by teachers within the filter parameters are included in the calculation. A new method for evaluating the hardware has been developed and implemented on this website. The evaluation is separated into 10 categories. These categories are listed below with a description.

Clarity

Is the interface simple and clear?

Cost

Is the cost of the hardware worth it?

Curriculum

Are curriculum resources readily obtainable?

Engagement

Does the system engage and motivate the intended audience of learners? Is it fun to use?

Error Avoidance

Does the program avoid errors where possible?

Feedback

Does the system provide timely and constructive feedback?

Non-Threatening

Does the system appear threatening? Do users feel safe in the knowledge that they can experiment without losing data?

Technical

General issues with the hardware.

Time

Do teachers have the necessary time to learn to use and teach the system effectively?

Visibility

Does the user always know what is happening on the system? Is it simple to navigate to other parts of the system?
